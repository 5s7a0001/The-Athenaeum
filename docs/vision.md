# Vision Document - The Athenaeum

The Athenaeum is a dark academia productivity workspace. It is designed to look and feel like an interactive 17th-century Dutch still-life painting. 

## Design Philosophy

The Athenaeum rejects traditional SaaS styling, neon colors, control boards, and glassmorphic dashboards. Instead, it recreates a tactile, candlelit scholar's desk where planning becomes an intentional daily ritual.

### 1. Tactile Objects as Interface
* **The Typewriter:** The mechanical heart of the workspace. It types and houses today's daily agenda. The paper physically scrolls upward from the carriage.
* **The Stack of Books:** The navigation. Spines are gold-leaf stamped with *Monthly Goals*, *Weekly Goals*, *Archive*, and *Settings*. Clicking opens the book to flip pages.
* **The Permanent Parchment:** The calendar. Always resting on the bottom-left of the desk. Clicking dates lets you schedule events, tasks, and deadlines.
* **The Teacup:** The Focus Mode. Clicking the marble chalice fades the desk into warm shadow, spotlighting your active task and countdown timer.

### 2. Time-Based Architecture
* **The Past (The Archive):** Preserved in a historic ledger. Contains immutable, frozen snapshots of past daily agendas.
* **The Present (The Typewriter):** Today's active daily agenda. Recreated at 5:00 AM each morning and typed out letter-by-letter as a ritual. Once generated, the print remains stable and acts as an interactive journal sheet.
* **The Future (The Calendar):** A Month-view calendar parchment sheet laying flat on the desk. This is the only place to navigate future dates, schedule tasks, and track upcoming deadlines.

## Visual & Atmospheric Aesthetics

* **Chiaroscuro Lighting:** High contrast shadows, warm candlelit glows casting light from the top-left, and deep dark corners.
* **Materials:** Oil canvas paint strokes, textured parchment, polished obsidian-black typewriter metal, veined marble, aged leather book bindings, gold lettering, and dark wood grains.
* **Soundscapes:** Optional mechanical key strikes, satisfying carriage return bells, pen scratch strokes on paper, and background ambiance (fireplace crackling, rain against leaded glass, library silence).
